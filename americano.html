AMERICANO

                  

                      A type of coffee drink prepared by diluting
                                        an espresso shot with hot water at a 1:3 to 1:4 ratio,
resulting in a drink that retains
                                          the complex flavors of espresso, but in a lighter way.
                                             It's the perfect pick-me-up during any time of the day.
                                          Give it a try, and I'm sure you'll be blissfully caffeinated!
