MATCHA

                      It's made from finely ground green tea leaves
that are mixed with steamed milk.
                                           Unlike other teas, where you steep the leaves and strain
                                       them, matcha is made by whisking the powder into water
      or milk. It's the perfect pick-me-up
during any time of the day.
                                           Give it a try, and I'm sure you'll be blissfully caffeinated!
